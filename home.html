Home

Welcome to JoJo's Bizarre Archive, a fan made archive dedicated to JoJo's Bizarre Adventure.

JoJo's Bizarre Adventure, JJBA for short, is a shōnen and seinen manga. It follows the journeys of the Joestar family memeber across nine unique parts- 1 through 6 being canonical, whereas part 7 and 8 are stand alone parts. Our goal is to create an archive for all official material related to this ongoing series.

About The Author

Hirohiko Araki (荒木 飛呂彦, born June 7, 1960) is a Japanese manga artist. He is best known for his long-running series JoJo's Bizarre Adventure, which was first published in Weekly Shōnen Jump in 1987.
