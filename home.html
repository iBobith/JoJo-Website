Home


Welcome to JoJo's Bizarre Archive, a fan made archive dedicated to JoJo's Bizarre Adventure.


JoJo's Bizarre Adventure, JJBA for short, is a shōnen and seinen manga. It follows the journeys of the Joestar family memeber across nine unique parts- 1 through 6 following one canonical storyline, whereas parts 7 and 8 are stand alone parts, set in alternate universes. Our goal is to create an archive for all official material related to this ongoing series.



About the Author



Hirohiko Araki (荒木 飛呂彦, born June 7, 1960) is a Japanese manga artist. He is best known for his long-running series JoJo's Bizarre Adventure, which was first published in Weekly Shōnen Jump in 1987.
